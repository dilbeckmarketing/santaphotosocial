SANTA PHOTO EVENT SOCIAL SHARES | Option A

The images below are designed to be shared on Facebook, Instagram, and Twitter in support of Dilbeck's Santa Photo Events. Each office has their own social media share and are labeled below.

To download the images:
1 – Right-click on the image you wish to save.
2 – Click "Save picture as"…
3 – Navigate to the folder where you wish to save the image to, then click Save.
